About Us

Whether you want to gain lean muscle, lose weight, tone up, or you are happy as you are but would like a healthier lifestyle, Build-A-Bull Fitness in Seattle is the right choice for you. Everyone wants to feel comfortable in their own skin, therefore, even if you are working a busy schedule, finding it hard to meet your fitness goals or would like to get fit, Build-A-Bull gives you the flexibility to reach your full potential with a variety of different online and individual personal training plans.


When my clients start training, they see their time in the gym as something else to juggle. But they soon learn that the flexibility, nutritional coaching and support they receive has a positive knock-on effect into the rest of their lives. They're more productive at work, they're more present for their families and importantly, they start to like themselves again.

Facilities


Flex Training Gym is located in the Capitol Hill neighborhood of Seattle at the corner of Madison and 19th. 1920 East Madison Street, Seattle, WA;


  • No Membership Required

    • A membership is not required to workout with a personal trainer at Flex. All our personal trainers are independent—they each have their own style and technique of personal training.


  • All Experience Levels Welcomed

    • We welcome all levels of fitness. We offer a beginner’s program, extreme weight loss program, strength and conditioning, core training, circuit training, sport-specific training, bodybuilding, and pre-competition training.


  • Convenient Location

    • We provide the best and most experienced personal training in Seattle, centrally located in Capitol Hill with ample street parking.
